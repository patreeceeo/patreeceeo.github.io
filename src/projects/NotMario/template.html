How to play

First, find a friend who can share your keyboard, then fight!

The goal depends on who you're playing as. For "Mr. Red Overalls" you're trying to collect as many mushrooms as possible, but watch out for the poison ones! For "Dino King" you want Overalls to suffer as much as possible.

controls

About

This was originally made very quickly for an art show, based on Meth Meth Method's Super Mario in a Browser tutorial code. I refactored it to follow an ECS pattern, which made it easier for me to expiriment with changes to gameplay. The current game design was to fit the theme of the art show. I hope to use this as a basis for a more original browser-based mulitplayer platformer that can be played over the internet.
